Professional Experience Projects

Live Event Production & Stagehand Experience

Throughout my career in the live event industry, I worked as a stagehand supporting concerts, sporting events, and large-scale productions. My role involved helping crews prepare venues, organize equipment, and complete setups and breakdowns within strict timelines.

Live events require strong communication and teamwork because every person plays an important role in creating a successful production. I worked with different crews and departments to complete projects safely and efficiently while adapting to changing situations.

Skills Developed:

  • Teamwork and communication
  • Safety awareness in high-pressure environments
  • Problem-solving and quick decision-making
  • Time management and meeting deadlines
  • Equipment handling and organization

This experience helped build the foundation of my work ethic and taught me how preparation, organization, and collaboration are important in any project.
