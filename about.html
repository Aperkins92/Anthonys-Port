Who I Am

My name is Anthony, and I am a college student with a unique background that combines hands-on experience, creativity, and a desire to continue learning. Before returning to school, I spent many years working in the live event industry as a stagehand, where I helped build and support concerts, sporting events, and large-scale productions. Working behind the scenes taught me the importance of teamwork, communication, preparation, and staying calm while working in fast-paced environments with strict deadlines. My experience in event production helped shape who I am today. Every project required problem-solving, attention to detail, and the ability to work with different teams to accomplish a shared goal. Whether setting up equipment, helping manage production needs, or making sure events ran smoothly, I learned that success comes from dedication, reliability, and being willing to adapt when challenges arise. After stepping back from the industry, I decided to continue my education and develop new skills. As a student, I am focused on improving my abilities in technology, web development, writing, and critical thinking. Building my first portfolio website has allowed me to explore how websites are created while combining my personal experiences and professional goals into something I can share with others. Outside of school and work, I enjoy drawing, listening to music, following sports, gaming, and spending time developing my creativity. I believe creativity and problem-solving go hand in hand, whether I am working on a project, learning a new skill, or finding solutions to everyday challenges. My goal is to continue growing by combining my years of real-world experience with the technical skills I am gaining through education. I want to create opportunities where I can use my teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving abilities while continuing to learn and take on new challenges. This portfolio represents my journey, my experiences, and the progress I am making toward my future goals.
