Who I Am

My name is Anthony, and I am a college student working toward building new skills and creating opportunities for my future. Before returning to school, I spent many years working in the live event industry as a stagehand, helping build concerts, sporting events, and large-scale productions.

Working behind the scenes in live events taught me the importance of teamwork, communication, preparation, and staying calm under pressure. Every production required coordination with different crews, attention to detail, and the ability to solve problems quickly in fast-paced environments.

My experience as a stagehand helped me develop a strong work ethic and taught me that every role contributes to the success of a project. Whether setting up equipment, supporting production teams, or helping complete projects on strict deadlines, I learned the value of reliability, safety, and working together toward a common goal.

I am currently continuing my education and developing skills in technology, web development, writing, and critical thinking. Building my personal portfolio website has allowed me to explore how websites are created while combining my creativity with technical skills. I enjoy learning new tools and finding ways to turn ideas into real projects.

Outside of school and work, I enjoy drawing, listening to music, following sports, gaming, and staying creative. These hobbies help me continue developing my problem-solving skills and allow me to express myself in different ways.

My goal is to continue growing by combining my real-world experience with the technical knowledge I am gaining through education. I want to create opportunities where I can use my teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving abilities while continuing to learn and take on new challenges.
